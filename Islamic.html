❓ Islamic Finance FAQ

Is cryptocurrency halal?

Yes — most scholars agree crypto is halal if used as a medium of exchange and not for speculation/gharar. TMT complies as an asset-backed utility token.

What is riba?

Riba (interest/usury) is strictly forbidden. All our products have zero interest — profits come from asset trading, leasing, or profit-sharing.

What is gharar?

Excessive uncertainty. Our contracts avoid speculation — all assets and terms are clearly defined upfront.

Is TMT itself halal?

Yes — TMT functions as a digital asset, not a speculative vehicle. It's used for staking, RWA, and utility payments.
