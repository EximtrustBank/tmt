1. Abstract

Turkmen New Manat (TMT) is a next-generation digital asset built on the Solana blockchain using the advanced Token-2022 program. TMT represents a modernized, programmable equivalent of the Turkmen national currency, optimized for high-speed decentralized finance (DeFi), cross-border settlements, and real-world asset (RWA) tokenization.

3. Token-2022 Advanced Features

TMT utilizes Solana's Token-2022 program, which provides enterprise-grade features:

  • Confidential Transfers: Zero-knowledge proofs for private transactions
  • Transfer Hooks: Automated KYC/AML compliance checks on every transfer
  • Transfer Fees: Optional fee mechanism for ecosystem funding
  • Permanent Supply: Mint authority revoked — no inflation ever
  • On-chain Metadata: Token name, symbol, logo, and description stored on-chain
  • Memo Field: Institutional compliance with transfer references

4. Tokenomics

The total supply of 100 trillion TMT is fixed and fully distributed at launch. Distribution:

  • Community & Ecosystem (60%): Airdrops, staking rewards, liquidity incentives
  • Development Fund (20%): Protocol development, security audits, infrastructure
  • Marketing & Listings (10%): CEX listings, marketing campaigns, partnerships
  • Team & Advisors (10%): 4-year vesting with 1-year cliff

5. Use Cases & Ecosystem

  • DeFi Hub: Staking (8-15% APY), yield farming (up to 25% APY), lending/borrowing
