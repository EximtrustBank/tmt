    ❓ FAQ

    ❓ Why can't I buy TMT directly with manats?

    No international exchange accepts Turkmen manats. This is not specific to TMT token — it applies to any crypto asset on any exchange.

    ❓ Which method is safest?

    Method 1 (Bitget → Jupiter). You only work with a licensed exchange (Bitget) and an official DEX (Jupiter). No P2P or intermediaries.

    ❓ Do I need to pay taxes when buying TMT?

    Under Turkmenistan's 'Virtual Assets' law (since Jan 1, 2026), crypto ownership is not taxed. But consult a lawyer to be sure.
