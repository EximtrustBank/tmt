📜 History of Turkmen Manat

The Turkmen manat was introduced on November 1, 1993, replacing the Soviet and Russian ruble at 500 RUR = 1 TMM. On January 1, 2009, a new manat was introduced with a denomination of 5000 old manats = 1 new manat (TMT). In 2025, polymer banknotes of 1, 5, 10, and a new 200 manat note were issued, dedicated to the 30th anniversary of Turkmenistan's neutrality. The official dollar exchange rate has been artificially set at 3.5 TMT per USD since 2015, but the black market rate is more than 10x higher (≈40 TMT per USD as of 2021).
